Our Story

Founded in 2009 by master technician Mike Johnson, CoolAir HVAC began as a small family business with a simple mission: provide honest, reliable heating and cooling services that homeowners can trust.

Over the past 15 years, we've grown from a one-man operation to a team of certified professionals, but our core values remain unchanged. We believe in transparency, quality workmanship, and putting our customers first in everything we do.

Today, we're proud to serve over 2,000 satisfied customers across the metro area, maintaining their comfort year-round with energy-efficient solutions and 24/7 emergency service.
